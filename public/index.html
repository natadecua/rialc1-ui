Tree Group Information

Trees in the La Mesa Eco Park were classified into five (5) taxonomic groups:

Group 1: Rosids (544 trees)

Includes species like Samanea saman (Rain Tree), Swietenia mahagoni (Mahogany), Pterocarpus indicus (Narra), and many others.

Group 2: Basals (155 trees)

Includes species like Macaranga tanarius, Chrysophyllum cainito, and Terminalia catappa.

Group 3: Asterids (166 trees)

Includes species like Tectona grandis (Teak), Gmelina arborea (Yemane), and Vitex parviflora.

Group 4: Monocots (77 trees)

Includes various palm species like Adonidia merrillii and other monocotyledon plants.

Group 5: Others (109 trees)

Species that don't fit into the other taxonomic groups.
