About the Model

This UI visualizes a tree species identification model trained on LiDAR-derived features. It shows predictions vs. ground truth, training samples, and evaluation metrics. Replace the sample data by importing your own GeoJSON. The 3D modal simulates a point cloud view; integrate Potree for real data.
